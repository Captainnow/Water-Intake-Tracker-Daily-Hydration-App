# Droplet - Water Intake Tracker & Daily Hydration App

💧 **Droplet** is a highly interactive, futuristic, and beautiful web application designed to help you track your daily hydration goals with style. Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the app operates natively in the browser without any heavy frameworks.

## ✨ Features

- **Stunning UI/UX**: Enjoy a fully responsive, premium "glassmorphism" design with 60FPS fluid CSS animations, glowing neon accents, and beautiful background wave effects.
- **Light & Dark Mode**: Seamlessly switch between a deep cyberpunk dark mode and a crisp, frosted-glass light mode using the intuitive droplet switch toggle.
- **Smart Goal Calculator**: Automatically calculates your ideal daily water intake based on your body weight, local climate, and daily physical activity level.
- **Dynamic Greetings**: The app greets you based on the time of day and offers motivational messages ("You're halfway there! 💪" or "Overachiever! 🌊") as you log your water intake.
- **Reward Animations**: Hit your daily goal to trigger a satisfying, screen-wide confetti celebration animation!
- **Detailed History**: Visualize your hydration journey with a 7-day bar chart, a 28-day heatmap, streak tracking, and unlockable achievement milestones.
- **Privacy First**: All your logs, goals, and settings are securely saved to your browser's local storage. No data leaves your device.

## 🚀 Getting Started

Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser to start tracking your hydration immediately. You can also run it via a local server (e.g., `Live Server` extension in VSCode or Python's `http.server`).

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **HTML5** for structure
- **CSS3** (Custom Properties, Flexbox, Animations, Backdrop-Filter)
- **Vanilla JavaScript** (ES6+, LocalStorage, Notifications API)
